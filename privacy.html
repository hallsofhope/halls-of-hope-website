Privacy Policy

How we protect your information and respect your privacy.

Introduction

Halls of Hope ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website hallsofhope.com and interact with our content.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Email Address: When you download free resources, sign up for our email list, or contact us.
  • Name: When you submit a testimonial or contact us directly.
  • Purchase Information: When you buy digital products through Gumroad, payment processing is handled by Gumroad and we do not store your credit card details.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous analytics about how visitors use our site (pages visited, time spent, etc.).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver free resources and digital products you request.
  • Send you updates about new content, videos, and products (only if you opt in).
  • Respond to your questions, feedback, or support requests.
  • Improve our website and content based on anonymous usage patterns.
  • Display community testimonials (with your permission only).

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our business:

  • Gumroad: Processes payments for digital products. Gumroad has its own privacy policy governing your purchase data.
  • YouTube: Our videos are hosted on YouTube. Viewing our videos is subject to YouTube's and Google's privacy policies.
  • Vercel: Hosts our website. Vercel may collect basic server logs.
  • Cloudflare: Provides DNS and security services for our domain.

Email Communications

If you provide your email address through our free resource downloads or email signup, we may send you occasional updates about new videos, products, and content. Every email includes an unsubscribe link so you can opt out at any time. We never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies for basic functionality and analytics. These help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our website and content are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
  • Unsubscribe from our email communications at any time.
  • Request removal of your testimonial from our website.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

Email: support@hallsofhope.com
